Waymouth & Ward Homecare Limited
Systems Implementation

"I have worked with Salome since 2012 and have been very lucky to have her support me, and the businesses and teams I have worked with over this time.
Salome has excellent attention to detail, with brilliant analysis skills and problem-solving skills. She is skilled at supporting all levels of teams from C-suite to branch staff, carers and support workers, and fits very well into any team environment. Salome is dedicated, to providing businesses, with cost-effective appropriate solutions and has a true understanding of the pressures and challenges of working in the health and social care environment.
Salome is very experienced working in highly pressurised private equity funded environments and supported with the transition of multiple acquisitions. This has included the implementation of operating systems (including Coldharbour, Staffplan Access People Planner, Care Planning and Care Compliance) across a range of brands, locations and service provision types, including configuration and set up, migration of data from current systems, training for all levels of employee, development of operating procedures, quality support and auditing.
Salome has also provided Quality and compliance support including writing policies and procedures, auditing branches and locations for CQC compliance, management of health and safety, GDPR and operational support with complaints, safeguarding and incident investigation management.
Salome is an asset to any team, and we are very thankful she continues to support our businesses with a range of services. " - Nicky Ward, Director
